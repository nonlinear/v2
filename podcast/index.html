Podcasts I listen to

February 23rd 2015 by Nicholas Frota on resources

I'm a major podcast listener for a while. Sadly, no podcast app gives me the ability to show my subscriptions to others, so below is a compilation of the ones I follow.

If you're new to all of it, I suggest using PocketCasts as a player. It's what I use.

Design

  • 99% Invisible short stories of man-made structures mostly invisible to us.
  • The Industry Radio Show sometimes too opinionate, but nice talk on the industry I work on.
  • Shoptalk less rants, more interviews, more hands-on advice. get a notepad.

Magick

  • C-Realm Podcast
  • The DisinfoCast with Matt Staggs
  • Occult Sentinel Podcast A podcast covering the hermetic arts, secret societies and various spiritual traditions.

Music

  • The Bunker Podcast best brooklyn techno party
  • GhostlyCast
  • Dave Clarke's White Noise
  • Verboten Transmissions
  • Wolf + Lamb Radio

Politics

  • Congressional Dish a weekly podcast highlighting corporate influence in the bills passed by Congress.
  • MSNBC Rachel Maddow
